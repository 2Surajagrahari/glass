The Story of the Lotus

The Golden Lotus is more than just a painting; it's a symbol of purity, enlightenment, and rebirth. Crafted over 40 hours, each line is carefully etched and filled with high-quality metallic paint that catches the light in a mesmerizing dance. The background is a deep, tranquil blue, representing the calm waters from which the lotus emerges. This piece is perfect for creating a serene and sophisticated atmosphere in your living room, bedroom, or meditation space.
